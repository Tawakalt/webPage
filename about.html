Who we are

My Kitchen is the number one restaurant in Nigeria, with
the finest experienced chefs who find cooking as a passion
rather than a job. Our goal is to re-define culinary
experiences in Nigeria by creating,
replicating and re-vamping the finest cuisines
from around the globe.





Vision

1. To help bring Nigeria up to par in the world culinary environment.
2. To offer world class culinary experience to Nigerians.
3. To cater for small and big events in a fancy way
4. To help open up and teach Nigerians the amazing and exciting ways food can be created to make art.
5.To help in the development of the youth by offering alternative career options. (Demands for chefs have tripled over the last 3 years)
